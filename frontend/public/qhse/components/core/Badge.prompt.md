Compact mono-set status / metadata pill — semantic tones plus the GB 6441 hazard grades.

```jsx
<Badge tone="accent">实时采集</Badge>
<Badge tone="success" variant="dot">已闭环</Badge>
<Badge tone="grade1">I 级隐患</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral | accent | success | warning | danger | info | grade1–grade4`. Variants: `soft` (default), `dot` (glowing status dot), `outline`. Sizes `sm | md`.
